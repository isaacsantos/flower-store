# Product: Petal & Bloom

An online flower store storefront. It showcases curated bouquets and flower arrangements, with a focus on same-day delivery and freshness.

## Key Features
- Hero banner with promotional messaging and CTAs
- Product carousel fetching live inventory from an external API (`https://product-43vr.onrender.com/api/products`)
- Navigation with anchor-based routing (Home, Shop, About, Contact)
- Footer with newsletter signup

## Brand Identity
- Name: Petal & Bloom
- Tone: Warm, romantic, nature-inspired
- Tagline style: "Fresh · Handcrafted · Delivered"
